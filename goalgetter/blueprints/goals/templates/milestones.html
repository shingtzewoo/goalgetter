Milestones

Annual goals can sometimes be big and overwhelming
We're going to break them down into manageable and achievbable chunks called milestones
Each milestone is aimed to be achieved at the end of 4 months, so in a year you should have 3 milestones

Milestones also have to be

Specific: be clear about what needs to be done
Measurable: defined criteria to measure success
Achievable: it has to be an outcome you can directly influence
Realistic: make sure you have the time, resources, and ability to succeed
Time: specific time periods to hold yourself accountable
